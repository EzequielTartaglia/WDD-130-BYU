Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the website is to invite the people to try rafting adventures in an natural reserve named "White Water Rafting (WWR)". Also, make people aware about the importance of pure and natural hydration without waste or harmful chemicals to improve the stamina of the people, for example to do sports. (Share information and sell a product/service)

Audience

The type of person who would visit this site will be an audience of young adults and adults, since 18 years old, with natural ideals and desires to live a healthy life. (Way to usually access to the site: Desktop and mobile device)
